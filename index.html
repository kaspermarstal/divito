About

Morten Jeppesen from PressConnect and Michael Kristiansen from Kristiansen+Partners have joined forces and launched DIVITO as a strategic cooperation, in response to what they assess to be an increased integration of financial communication, strategic press work, investor relations, preservation of political interests and social media.

By unifying their specialized competences within these core areas, Morten and Michael offer complete solutions for listed companies, companies headed for a stock market listing, large unlisted companies, as well as venture capital funds.

Morten Jeppesen

Morten is educated as a journalist and has significant experience from both the newspaper and communications industry, for instance as Senior Vice President, Group Communication & Public Affairs at PFA Pension, Falck and Ejendomsforeningen Danmark (Danish Property Federation), Editor-in-Chief at Økonomisk Ugebrev (online financial newspaper), and Financial Editor at Børsen (Business Daily).

He is also the author of the book “Blændværk” concerning the collapse of Roskilde Bank. Morten is today the founder and owner PressConnect, a communications and PR advisory firm, as well as a columnist and member of the advisory board at the online news magazine Inside Business.

Michael Kristiansen

Michael has a journalistic background but has predominantly worked as an advisor and commentator within PA, communications and the political sphere. He has worked as a Special Advisor for the former Danish Prime Minister Anders Fogh Rasmussen, External Press Advisor at Danske Bank and as Head of Communications at Kristiansen Strategy and KristiansenUlveman.

He is also the co-author of the books “Poul Schlüter – en biografi” and “Niels Helveg Petersen – Manden og magten”, as well as the co-director of the documentary “Kandidaterne.”

Michael is currently a columnist at Børsen (Business Daily), political commentator in Tirsdagsanalysen at TV2/News, Chairman of the Board at Geopark Odsherred, Board Member at Dragsholm Slot and partner and communications advisor at Kristiansen+Partners.
